# SailTalk — Privacy Policy
**Last updated: 6/12/2025**  
Developed by **Tucanoo Solutions Ltd.**

SailTalk (“the App”) is an offline educational application designed to help users learn English–Spanish nautical terminology. Tucanoo Solutions Ltd. (“we”, “us”, “our”) is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy describes what information we collect, how we use it, and the rights available to you.

By using SailTalk, you agree to the terms described here.

## 1. Overview
SailTalk is designed to function entirely **offline**.  
We do **not** collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data.

The app does not require an account, does not track user behaviour, and does not include advertising.

---

## 2. Information We Do Not Collect
We do **not** collect any of the following:

- Personal information (name, email, address, phone number)  
- Usage data or analytics  
- Location data  
- Contact lists  
- Photos, media, or files  
- Search history or learning progress  
- Voice or audio recordings  
- Cookies or tracking identifiers  
- Device identifiers (IDFA, GAID)

We do not sell, rent, or share any user data because no personal data is collected at all.

## 3. Information Processed Locally (On Your Device Only)

### 3.1 Text-to-Speech (TTS) Playback
SailTalk uses your device’s built-in Text-to-Speech engine to generate audio for pronunciations.

- No voice recordings are captured.  
- No audio is sent to any server.  
- All processing occurs locally on your device.

### 3.2 Local Learning Data
Any favourites, quiz results, or learning progress (present or future versions) are stored only on your device and are never transmitted.
